Reactions

Presentation

Rules and Information

Create a presentation (Prezi, Powerpoint, Educreations, your choice) identifying which atoms will react with each other so that we will know to store them away from each other
For example:

  • Nitrogen (5 valence electrons) will react with Boron (3 valence electrons) and they should not be stored near each other.

  • Nitrogen (5 valence electrons) will also react with Aluminum (3 valence electrons) and they should not be stored near each other.


Details

Your presentation must include 1 atom from group 1 (the Alkali Metals), group 2 (the Alkali Earth Metals), group 7 (the Halogens) and group 8 (the Noble Gases). Your presentation must include diagrams of the atom showing the valence electrons. Your presentation must include at least 8 different reactions; 4 of those reactions must include elements exclusively from the first three rows of the periodic table.

How will this be graded?

The rubric for the Content Knowledge grade is on the back of this paper

Finished?

Your presentation should be saved in your Edmodo backpack and then turned in to Ms. Komnick via Edmodo. You should also save your presentation in at least one other way (flash, cloud, etc.) to ensure that it is not lost.

You may elect to complete this project by yourself, or you may elect to have on partner. If you choose to have a partner, both parties will receive the same grade.
